Plant a Seed

Growing a garden can be a fun and rewarding activity! To get started, follow the steps listed below.

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

  • Seeds
  • Potting Soil
  • Pot or planter
  • Garden tools (trowel, gloves, rake, shovel, etc.)

Step 2: Choose Your Location

Choose a spot in your yard that gets plenty of sunshine and has good drainage. Avoid spots that are too shady or too wet.

Step 3: Prep Your Soil

Loosen up the soil in your chosen spot by using a shovel or trowel to turn it over. Remove any large rocks or debris that you find.

Step 4: Plant Your Seeds

Dig small holes for each seed, and carefully place each one in its own hole. Cover the seeds up with the soil, and press lightly on top.

Step 5: Water and Fertilize

Water the soil until it's moist and evenly damp. Apply a layer of fertilizer to help your plants grow quickly and healthily.

Step 6: Monitor Growth

Keep an eye on your garden every day to make sure it's getting enough water and nutrients. If you spot any weeds, be sure to pull them up right away.

Step 7: Enjoy!

Once your plants have grown and started to flower or produce fruit, it's time to sit back and enjoy the fruits of your labor!
